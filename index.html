Welcome to Jonathan Ezeugo's Bio Page

From an early age, I've loved figuring out how things work and what positive use can be
made of them. This attracted me to the field of Engineering and a choice for Mechanical
Engineering to design components, systems and processes to solve problems. Much of my career
has been in oil and gas drilling and measurements, helping a vast array of prospectors optimize
their hydrocarbon yield. I chose to advance my knowledge and skill set in Mechanical Engineering
in such areas as Systems Engineering, including Subsea Engineering.

I love to learn new things, and that is a skill that has always been invaluable, especially in these days.
Currently I have opted to explore my interest in data processing, analytics and applications, in order to
blend these skill sets with prior knowledge towards the goal of providing solutions to key technical and
data analytics challenges in diverse fields and industries. My journey has taken me to the Rice University
Data Analytics Boot Camp, where I am undertaking special topics in Data Analytics and Visualizations.

I am excited with the team at Rice and am eagerly looking forward to the adventures this will bring.
